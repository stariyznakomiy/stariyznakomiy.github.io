About us

We are a international community of people united by the goal of raising the level of consciousness of humanity through changing ourselves and the world around us. Knowledge is passed down from teacher to disciple from generation to generation and has its roots in ancient times.

Together, we reside in learning centers located across various parts of the world. Through our collective experiences of inner growth, we cultivate an environment brimming with elevated vibrations.

We establish a unique energy field during our retreats, facilitating gentle yet profound inner transformations. This enables participants to access awakening experiences and reach new levels of consciousness.

We create a special energy field, which helps to effectively and gently go through inner transformations, allows experiencing experiences of awakening and reaching other levels of consciousness.

The retreat serves as the initial step in our comprehensive spiritual realization methodology, guiding individuals away from the realm of illusions, the matrix, or Samsara. For those who desire to continue their journey, the Paradanta Meditation 30-day course is available, with opportunities to become students of our school. Our ultimate aim is to establish harmonious communities worldwide, where people live in symbiosis with nature and one another. Currently, we have two study centers in Europe and Russia, housing over 100 individuals who work, practice, and live together as they strive for higher levels of consciousness.

As a natural consequence of our deep presence in the present moment, we witness external changes occurring as well. Our collective efforts contribute to reducing natural disasters, conflicts, violence, and inequality, while promoting ecological restoration and raising the vibrational frequency of our planet.

It is important to note that our methodology remains independent of any political, national, or religious affiliations or beliefs.

Through the guidance of an Enlightened teacher
Genadiy Givin

Genadiy, born in Riga, Latvia, was an exceptionally healthy child, demonstrating his strength through swimming and martial arts such as boxing, sambo, and karate and later on by becoming a master of sports. His path diverged during high school when he first encountered spiritually awakened individuals. One of these enlightened souls, working in cosmonautics, became Genadiy's guide, leading him to full spiritual realization by the age of 27.

Now, Genadiy's focus is on sharing his wisdom. Students from Germany, Latvia, Lithuania, Ukraine, America, and beyond, come seeking his guidance, which he provides through retreats, Paradanta meditation courses, Satsangs, and continous education as part of our school.

Today, his mission is to share this knowledge worldwide, helping others elevate their consciousness. He leads all retreats both on the energetic level and personally answering questions from participants.

His teachings stem from an ancient lineage of mysticism from Western Europe and has been referred to as the School of Primordial Sound.
